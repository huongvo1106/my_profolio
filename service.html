Volunteer Activity

RSB Cambridge Boxing for Parkinson's

RSB is a non-profit organization helping seniors with Parkison’s Disease.
The purpose of this organization’s project was to renovate an old and dusty building into a gym for Parkinson’s patients to exercise, practice muscle movements, and socialize with others who suffer from the disorder.
In March, I led a group of 30 students to the RSB Gym to volunteer our services. While there, we vacuumed the rooms and loaded heavy supplies into the building. We cleaned all the walls, took down old stickers, and then painted the walls from black to maroon.

Bella Buddies Service Dog Training

Bella Buddies is a nonprofit organization that trains puppies to become service dogs for military personnel. People who have served in the military can even spend the night in building hubs and play with the puppies for free.
Last November, I led a group of 40 students to Buffalo, Texas where the purpose of the project was to help complete the building hubs and to clean up the land. While there, we trimmed branches, cleaned up fences, loaded heavy furniture into the hubs, cleaned up dog poop, and loved on and played with the puppies.
